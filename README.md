# Real-Time-Chat-App

Pitch:
"Lonely? Connect with someone who truly understands. Our app helps people have real conversations, make friends, and feel less alone anytime, anywhere."

Project Description:
"The Loneliness Chat App is designed to help people combat loneliness by connecting them with others looking for meaningful conversations. Users can chat in real-time, share thoughts, and build friendships in a safe and friendly environment. The app focuses on empathy, understanding, and human connection, making it easy for anyone feeling isolated to reach out and feel heard."
